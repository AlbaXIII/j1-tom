J1-Tom - The home of english-language Japanese Football

J1 League - the highest level of football in Japan

Tomodachi (ともだち)- Japanese word for friend

Since travelling to Japan in 2023, I have grown a keen interest in Japanese football. The fan culture, atmosphere and presentation quickly drew me in - to say nothing of the fast, skilful football on show.

J1-Tom is my attempt to create a simple English-language portal for those, like me, who want an easy way to learn more about the beautiful game in Japan.
